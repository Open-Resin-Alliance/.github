# Open Resin Alliance &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; [![Discord Link](https://discordapp.com/api/guilds/1281738817417777204/widget.png?style=shield)](https://discord.gg/beFeTaPH6v)

We're a group of hobbyists and developers from around the globe, joined in our dedication to advancing the field of resin-based 3D printing through open source collaboration.

We believe that by sharing knowledge and resources, we can accelerate innovation and make high-quality 3D printing accessible to everyone.

## Projects
> :information_source: Some of these projects have grown and moved from TheContrappostoShop to the ORA, to better serve the community at large.

All of our projects, primarily software and reference designs for open-source resin 3d printing, are here on GitHub.  
Below are a few of our most impactful projects.

#### [Odyssey](https://github.com/Open-Resin-Alliance/Odyssey)
> Resin printer backend software

#### [Orion Frontend](https://github.com/Open-Resin-Alliance/Orion)
> Resin printer frontend software

#### DragonFruit Slicer (_Coming Soon_)
> Fully Open-Source slicer for resin printing
